# Wizz Lagonisi - Restaurant Information

## About Us
Wizz Lagonisi is a premier dining destination located in Lagonisi, offering a unique blend of Mediterranean cuisine and modern vibes. We are known for our stunning sea views, vibrant atmosphere, and exceptional service.

## Policies
- **Reservations:** Recommended for dinner and weekends. Can be made online or via phone.
- **Dress Code:** Smart Casual. Beachwear is allowed only in the beach bar area during the day.
- **Pets:** Small pets are allowed in the outdoor seating area.
- **Parking:** Valet parking is available.

## Events
- **Live Music:** Every Friday and Saturday night starting at 10:00 PM.
- **Private Parties:** We host weddings, birthdays, and corporate events. Contact us for packages.
- **Sunset Sessions:** DJ sets every Sunday afternoon during summer.

## FAQ
- **Do you have vegan options?** Yes, we have a dedicated vegan section in our menu.
- **Is the restaurant wheelchair accessible?** Yes, all areas are accessible via ramps.
- **Do you offer delivery?** No, we currently offer dine-in and takeaway only.
